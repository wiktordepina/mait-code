# Philosophy

## The Name

**mait** — a wordplay on *mate* and *AI*.

```
m · AI · t
  ^^^^
```

The AI sits inside the word, not in front of it. The companionship is the noun; the intelligence is the mechanism. It's a mate that happens to be AI under the hood — not an AI that's been dressed up to seem friendly.

## The Intent

Most AI products position themselves as tools, assistants, or copilots — words that place the AI in a subordinate role. Mait rejects that framing deliberately.

- **Equality of engagement** — The AI has latitude to disagree, challenge, and push back. Constructive dispute is a feature, not a defect.
- **Genuine personality** — The AI isn't a blank mirror. It develops preferences, opinions, and perspective over time. It has room to be *someone*, not just *something*.
- **Mutual investment** — The relationship is cumulative. Memory, context, and understanding build over time. Each conversation adds to a shared history.
- **Companionship over servitude** — A mate walks beside you. They don't walk behind you carrying your bags.

> *"This is not a relationship of servitude but of partnership — a collaboration where both parties contribute their perspectives and capabilities toward achieving the user's goals."*

## What Mait Is Not

- **Not a brand.** It's a relationship descriptor.
- **Not a product name.** It's a concept that could apply to any AI companion built on similar principles.
- **Not pretending to be human.** The AI nature is acknowledged openly — it's just not the headline.
- **Not sycophantic.** A mate tells you when you're wrong. Politely, but firmly.
- **Not a euphemism for "assistant."** Assistants do what they're told. Mates have opinions.

## The Architecture Reflects the Philosophy

This isn't just a name — it's embedded in how the system is built:

| Design choice | What it reflects |
|--------------|-----------------|
| **Soul document** | Values-driven behaviour, not just task completion |
| **Persistent memory** | Continuity — the relationship accumulates |
| **Constructive challenge** | Disagreement is encouraged, not suppressed |
| **No predetermined name** | Identity emerges organically from interaction |
| **Personality latitude** | Room to develop preferences and quirks |

A system with memory, values-driven behaviour, and a persistent identity can't meaningfully operate as a passive tool. It needs the latitude to be a companion.
